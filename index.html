Introduction

Welcome to the Best Looking Man in Illinois Contest! This annual event recognizes the most handsome and attractive men in the state. If you think you have what it takes to be the next Best Looking Man in Illinois, read on to learn more about the contest and how to enter.

Rules

  1. Contestants must be male and at least 18 years old.
  2. Contestants must be legal residents of Illinois.
  3. Contestants must submit a recent photograph of themselves along with their entry form.
  4. A panel of judges will select the top 10 finalists based on the photographs submitted.
  5. The top 10 finalists will be invited to a live event where they will be judged on their appearance, personality, and public speaking skills.
  6. The winner will be chosen by the judges and announced at the live event.

Entry Form

To enter the contest, please complete the form below and attach a recent photograph of yourself. The entry fee is $50. Payment can be made through PayPal or by mailing a check or money order to the address provided on the form.
